# FlirtIQ — Short-Form Video Scripts

> 15 ready-to-film scripts for TikTok / Reels / YouTube Shorts.
> **Rule:** the first 1 second decides everything. Lead with the hook on screen + face or chat visible immediately. Post 2–3/day. Repurpose the same video across all 3 platforms.

**Format key:**
- 🎬 = on-screen visual
- 💬 = text overlay
- 🎤 = voiceover / spoken
- ⏱️ target length: 12–25 seconds

## PRODUCTION TIPS
- **No fancy gear** — screen recording + your phone camera is enough. Authentic > polished in this niche.
- **Trending audio** — always use a sound that's currently trending (check the TikTok "trending" tab). This alone can 5x reach.
- **Captions on screen** — most watch muted.
- **Hook in first frame** — never a slow intro. Text overlay must be readable at 0s.
- **CTA every time** — "link in bio" + point at it.
- **Batch film** — shoot 10 videos in one session, post over a week.
- **Repost winners** — if one hits, remake it 3 different ways.

## WHAT TO EXPECT
- 80% of videos get <1k views. Normal. Keep posting.
- You need ~30–50 videos before the algorithm "understands" your account.
- One viral video (100k+) can drive thousands of installs overnight — have the app + payments ready (they are).

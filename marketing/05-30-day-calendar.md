# FlirtIQ — 30-Day Done-For-You Content Calendar

> Your daily playbook. Each day tells you exactly what to film, the on-screen hook, the caption, hashtags, and when to post.
>
> **How to use:** Each morning, open today's row. Film the video (use shot lists in `01-video-scripts.md`), add the on-screen text, post with the caption + hashtags at the suggested time. Cross-post the same video to TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
>
> **Daily cadence:** 1 "hero" video per day minimum (this calendar). If you can do 2-3, repost top performers or test a second hook. Reply to every comment in the first hour.
>
> **Posting times (local):** TikTok 6pm or 9pm · Reels 11am or 7pm · Shorts 3pm or 8pm. Pick what fits your schedule — consistency matters more than the exact minute.

## RULES THAT MAKE THIS WORK
1. **Hook in frame 1.** The on-screen text must be readable at 0 seconds. No slow intros.
2. **Trending audio every time.** Check TikTok's trending tab; swap in a current sound. This alone can multiply reach.
3. **Captions on screen** (most watch muted).
4. **Reply to every comment in the first hour** — it signals the algorithm.
5. **When one video pops (>10k), remake it 2-3 ways immediately.** Ride the wave.
6. **Don't judge by day 7.** The account needs ~30 videos before the algorithm "gets" it. This calendar gets you there.
7. **Repost across all 3 platforms.** Export from CapCut (no watermark), not TikTok's save button.

## TRACK THIS WEEKLY
- Views per video (which hooks win?)
- Profile visits → link clicks → installs
- Free → paid conversion
- Double down on the format that converts, cut what doesn't.

## AFTER DAY 30
- You'll know your 3-5 best-performing formats. Stop guessing — make variations of those.
- Start the **creator-seeding** play (DM micro-creators, free credits for a video).
- Consider a small paid boost ($5-10/day) ONLY on a video that already went semi-viral organically.

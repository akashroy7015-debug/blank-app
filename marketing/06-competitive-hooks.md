# FlirtIQ — Competitive Positioning & Ad Hooks

> Based on competitor research of Rizz (market leader) and RizzGPT. These hooks attack their weaknesses where FlirtIQ is genuinely stronger. Use them in ads, landing copy, app store text, and video captions.

## The 4 wedges (where competitors are weak, you're strong)

| Their weakness (real user complaints) | FlirtIQ's edge |
|---|---|
| "Suggestions feel generic/cheesy" | **Context field** — tell FlirtIQ your goal, get tailored replies (not templates) |
| "Charged after canceling / billing bugs / free tier cut to 1/week" | **Honest pricing** — 3 free every day, clean billing, cancel anytime |
| "English-only, misses cultural nuance" | **10+ languages** incl. Hinglish — replies in your vibe |
| "Only one screenshot, no way to add context" | **Add context** + screenshot together |

## APP STORE / REVIEW-RESPONSE ANGLES
- Lead the store description with: **"Honest pricing — 3 free every day, cancel anytime, no surprise charges."** (Directly counters the #1 reason people rage-quit competitors.)
- When you get reviews mentioning other apps' billing issues, the differentiator writes itself.

---

## DON'T COPY THIS (competitor traps to avoid)
- ❌ Don't cut your free tier to bait subscriptions — their #1 source of 1-star reviews. Your generous free tier IS the marketing.
- ❌ Don't add NSFW/Roast modes carelessly — they risk app-store rejection (Mature rating + policy review). Only if you're ready for the compliance.
- ❌ Don't make billing confusing. Their broken "subscription detection" (charging active subscribers again) is a cautionary tale — your Paddle setup is clean, keep it that way.

## ONE THING THEY DO THAT YOU SHOULD CONSIDER LATER
**A keyboard extension** (generate replies inside the dating app without switching) is Rizz's hero feature and a real retention driver. It's a significant build (native iOS/Android keyboard), so park it — but it's the #1 feature to add once you have traction and revenue.

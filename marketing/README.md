Everything you need to launch and grow FlirtIQ. Start here.

## The one thing that matters
For an AI dating/rizz app, **short-form video drives ~80% of installs.** The product is built and payments work — distribution is now the entire game. Post 2–3 demo videos every day. Lean into the multilingual angle (Hinglish/Spanish) — almost no competitor does it.

## Quick-start (this week)
1. Claim social handles `@flirtiq` everywhere
2. Film 15 videos from `01-video-scripts.md`
3. Submit `flirtiq.online/sitemap.xml` to Google Search Console
4. Post 2–3 videos/day, reply to every comment
5. Plan the Product Hunt launch for next Tuesday/Wednesday
